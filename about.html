About

BearTooth is a nonprofit organization focused on funding education programs in underpriveledged cities all over the globe. Here at Beartooth, we beleive basic education is a necessity for societies to thrive. Basic education is the foundation of a functional and productive society. In our efforts to provide this to those who don't have the resources, we create opportunities for jobs within our organization for those interested in getting into public service. We hire students from all over the world who share our values, offering experience, competitive pay, and scholarships.

How we started

Beartooth was started when Azucena Risco was still in college. She has always been an activist when it comes to education, and when she realized she had the resources and the capacity to make a difference, she started Beartooth. Her goal from the start was to provide education to underpriviledged corners of the world. In 2014, Beartooth took shape, and less than a year later the organization had raised 70,000 dollars for a project in Madagascar. A facility and books were purchased, and a staff was formed. That was the first school that Beartooth formed, and the partners at Beartooth have been working hard ever since.

Future Plans

Beartooth has been looking into starting projects in Bolivia. We are in the process of meeting with local government officials to sort out the details, and after that we will be organizing a few fundraising events. For this project, we are joining with another organization from Peru. Our goal is to build several schools, as well as hire staff members. The plan will provide education to areas that hold well over 20,000 people. We are hopeful to make this work.
